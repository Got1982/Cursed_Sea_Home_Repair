All jobs are backed with a 30 day parts only repair guarantee. What does this mean for you? This means that if we do a job for you, and something breaks that is not caused by human interaction within thirty (30) days, we will come out and repair it at cost. No Labor or service charges will apply. Call or email us to schedule and appointment or for service inquiries. Prices will be based on cost of materials and estimated time to complete the job. We must be able to see the job that needs to be done to make and accurate estimate. Some jobs can be estimated over the phone through video chat, others may require an onsite estimate. Large jobs are subject to re-negotiation, as they may take more or less time and materials than expected. We are always available to answer questions you may have,at our customer service email.
